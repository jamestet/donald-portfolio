The problem

Most AI agents are either over-constrained chatbots or unconstrained security risks.

The build

A local multi-agent system: a main operator monitors state and asynchronously dispatches high-compute work to specialized worker modes (Research Assistant, Content Strategist) — without blocking the UI or telemetry.

Safety by design

Strict READ_ONLY boundary — no execution, no wallet signing, no writes outside sandboxed drafts — with a persisted HALT kill-switch and a SIEM-style event log. A SQLite prediction/audit ledger (in progress) tracks recommendations and provenance.

The build

A Python webhook ingests call-end reports from a Vapi voice agent, parses the lead, logs it to a CRM/CSV, and fires real-time multi-channel alerts (email, SMS, Telegram).

Deployed & compliant

Runs behind a Cloudflare tunnel with environment-gated integrations; inbound-only by design for TCPA compliance. Templatized to resell across contractor verticals.
